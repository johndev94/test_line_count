About Us

Since 2023, CinemaSol has strived to go to the furthest extent in order to satisfy our customers and service users. We do this through keeping our prices in check, offering accessible cinema seating and through donations to local charities in order to support our local community in Letterkenny.

Thank you for making CinemaSol your cinema. We are nothing without our patrons and community. We look forward to serving you and exceeding your expectations. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
